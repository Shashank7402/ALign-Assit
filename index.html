OUR SERVICES

Align Assist is a billing company offering advanced and high-quality practice management services to medical and non-medical specialties including physiotherapy, orthopedics, dermatology, neurology, cardiology, oncology, internal medicine, gynecology, and many more.

01. MEDICAL BILLING & CODING

Revive your financial performance with expert billing and coding professionals backed by over 10 years of combined experience. Accurate billing ensures faster reimbursements and prevents costly claim rejections.

02. COMPREHENSIVE CHART AUDITING

Maximize returns and reduce accounts receivable through expert chart audits. We ensure correct coding, regulatory compliance, and adherence to payer-specific rules for stronger financial outcomes.

03. REVENUE CYCLE MANAGEMENT

Complete revenue cycle management from patient check-in to final payment. With certified coders and a 99.9% claim acceptance rate, we manage claims, statements, follow-ups, and detailed reporting.

04. MANAGING WORKFLOW & TRAINING

Long-term operational improvement through staff training, compliance education, workflow optimization, and productivity enhancement to strengthen your practice.

05. ACCOUNT RECEIVABLES & DENIAL MANAGEMENT

Recover lost revenue with rigorous AR follow-ups and denial resolution. We identify root causes, resubmit claims efficiently, and ensure timely reimbursements to protect your cash flow.
